Week 1

Lesson 1: Introduction to Algebraic Operations

-   Introduction to the course and syllabus
-   Review of real numbers and their properties (commutative, associative, distributive)
-   Operations with real numbers (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
-   Order of operations and evaluating expressions

Lesson 2: Simplifying Algebraic Expressions

-   Simplifying algebraic expressions by combining like terms
-   Simplifying algebraic expressions using the distributive property
-   Simplifying algebraic expressions with variables and exponents

In each lesson, you can use a combination of lecture, examples, and practice problems to help students understand and apply the concepts. You can also assign homework to reinforce the topics covered in class. Additionally, you can use interactive tools such as online quizzes or games to make the learning process more engaging and interactive.
